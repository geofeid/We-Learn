Our classes

At this momment we offer 4 choices for learning the Spanish language. They are designed in a way that all will find a place to fit in

Spanish intermediate 1

If you already know the basics of the Spannish languge then, this is the course that you are looking for.

This 40 hours long class will give you the chance to enhance your grammar nad vocabulary in Spannish. This class is ideal for all ages and will upskill all students accordingly to their needs. This is the step you have to take if you want to start have conversations without having to ask the other person to speak in a more simple way. Spanish intermediate 2

If you already an intermediate student of the Spannish languge but not fluent yet then, this is the course that you are looking for.

This 40 hours long class will give you the chance to enhance your listening and speaking in Spannish. This class is ideal for all ages and will upskill all students accordingly to their needs. This is the step you have to take if you want to start speaking fluenty and have quality conversations with native Spannish people. By completing this course you will be a step closer in becoming a Spannish master. Each learning group will be of 6-8 people max.

Spanish advances

If you already speak fluenty Spannish and want some preparation for your certification then, this is the course that you are looking for.

You will be tested in Grammar, Vocabulary, Reading, Writting and Speaking. This class is ideal for all ages and will crusial for both people shitting the exam for the first time and those who want to retake it in order to pass this time.
